COVID CARE CENTRES

Families living in single room houses with no access to isolation facilities stand no chance when the third wave strikes.

With the help of the local government, KHUSHII is converting schools to provide free patient stay, nursing care, food, oxygen, medicine and online doctor consultations.

Your support is critical to help communities living in urban slums recover & break the chain.

MENTAL HEALTH

There is a 50% increase in distress calls with complains of distress and abuse from children ever since COVID started.

Your donation will help us take care of underprivileged children who under extreme stress and anxiety are prone to sexual abuse, child labour and violence.

EDUCATION CONTINUES

Millions of children with little or no access to technology will drop out of school as COVID impacts their families. A loss of 440 billion* US$ is predicted because of school closures.

KHUSHII's Adapt Impact Centres has helped 60,000 children continue their education by adapting to overcome on ground challenges.

Help bridge the learning gap so no child drops out of school.

EMERGENCY SUPPLIES

Loss of livelihood has driven millions of families into desperation and they are unable to get by. This is also affecting children and their future.

KHUSHII has so far distributed 1730 dry ration packs, home isolation kits but there is so much more that remains to be done.

We need your support to help the underprivileged get by as we prepare for the third wave.
